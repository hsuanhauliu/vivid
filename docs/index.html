Every memory, vivid and easy to find

Notable features

Six things Vivid does especially well — plus a lot more built in.

AI auto-tagging & smart search

Every photo and video is tagged automatically by what's in it — scenes, objects, places. Then search by meaning, in 100+ languages: type "dog on a beach" and find it instantly. On-device OCR makes text in screenshots and documents searchable too, and a rich set of filters narrows things down further. All on-device, nothing leaves your Mac.

Automatic backup & sync

Point Vivid at a folder — an external drive, iCloud Drive, Dropbox — and it keeps a live, one-way mirror of your library up to date automatically. Your files, always backed up.

Import, however you like

Drag and drop, watched folders that auto-import, direct URL downloads, upload straight from your phone, or bring in your whole iCloud Photos or Google Photos library.

Organize and view it your way

Albums, album groups, playlists, and folders keep your library tidy, and you can browse it however suits you — masonry, card, or list view, or the world map, plotting every geotagged photo and video on a map you can explore and multi-select.

Multiple workspaces

Manage more than one library side by side. Let Vivid organize your files on disk, or link an existing folder in place with zero copying — add, switch, and unlink workspaces anytime.

Private & native

No account, no cloud, no telemetry. A native Rust core, not a bundled browser — everything about your library stays on your Mac.

…and plenty more built in

Fast, native performance

A Rust core keeps browsing and searching snappy even on huge libraries.

Saved searches

Bookmark a keyword, filter, and scope combination and get back to it in one click.

Built-in video & audio players

A feature-rich native video player with frame-accurate scrubbing and variable speed, plus a full music player with queue control and gorgeous cover art.

Album groups

Nest related albums together so big libraries stay organized, not just flat lists.

Find duplicates & similar shots

Spot near-duplicates and burst shots automatically, then compare side by side to pick the keeper.

Quick video editing

Drag-to-trim, frame-by-frame scrubbing, GIF export, and instant frame capture — all without leaving your library.

World map multi-select

Marquee-drag or Ctrl+click to select pins in bulk, then hide or isolate them.

Localized in 11 languages

A fully native UI in English, Spanish, French, German, 繁體中文, 日本語, and more.
